PhishGraud:

PhishGraud is a site that predicts whether a URL is a phishing or legitimate one. The site uses a machine learning-based approach that predicts whether a URL is a phishing or legitimate one. It extracts 24 features from multiple websites' data to make accurate predictions, considering factors such as the frequency of http or https in the URL, the use of redirection, and the length of URLs.
